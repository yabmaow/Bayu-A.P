Projects

Malang Punk Attack

A documentary photography project focused on a punk music event in Malang, celebrating the spirit of resistance and solidarity within the local punk community. This project will capture the energy and expressions of performers and audience members alike, from intense stage performances to moments of togetherness behind the scenes. Through authentic portraits, this documentary aims to showcase the dynamics of punk culture in Malang, appreciating freedom of expression and presenting punk music as a powerful medium for rebellion and unity.

Graduation Photos

My photography approach captures key moments, showcasing the proud smiles of graduates in their caps and gowns, as well as the heartfelt embraces with family and friends. Each photo is thoughtfully composed to tell a story, encapsulating the essence of the graduation experience. From candid shots of joyous celebrations to portraits that reflect individual achievements, I aim to create a visual narrative that honors the dedication and hard work that led to this significant milestone.

Model Photography

I also specialize in model photography. This genre allows me to collaborate with models to create striking images that showcase their personalities and style. My approach involves working closely with models to capture authentic expressions and emotions, whether it's in a studio setting or an outdoor environment.
